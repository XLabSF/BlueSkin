Raise awareness. Take actions.

Build and grow a community interested in how climate change affects our personal health by providing a measurement tool.

Protection

WHO reports that solar radiation is considered as the main cause of skin cancer such as melanoma. The most vulnerable group are descendants of white Caucasians and their descendants, especially people living in high attitude with fair skin.

Risk Assessment

Provide a reliable and fast measurement tool to reduce a cost of premium insurance, hence offer an affordable financial protection for a majority of people, especially in a vulnerable group with a low and average income.

Prevention

Communicate with public audience by a practical and empirical evidence related to daily life, then evoke more consciousness about prevention and green behaviours towards climate change.
